About refuel


Our Story


Founded by a devoted lover of all things automotive and coffee connoisseur, Xander Poalses had a vision: to create a haven for car enthusiasts and coffee aficionados alike. refuel was born from the fusion of these two great passions, resulting in a unique establishment that offers an unparalleled experience for those who share our love for the open road and the perfect brew.



A Café Like No Other


At refuel, we've created a destination that goes beyond the ordinary. Our establishment isn't just a cafe; it's a lifestyle. Every corner of our space is adorned with the spirit of vintage racing, celebrating the timeless elegance of classic cars and the adrenaline-fueled excitement of the racetrack. Whether you're a seasoned gearhead or simply appreciate the aesthetic of these marvels of engineering, you'll find our cafe to be an ode to automotive artistry.



Savor the Perfect Brew


Indulge in the rich aroma and bold flavors of our carefully curated coffee menu. We offer a selection of hot beverages that caters to all preferences, from classic cappuccinos and lattes to more exotic delights like the indulgent café affogato. Each cup is crafted with precision and love, ensuring you experience the very essence of fine coffee in every sip.



Treats and Eats


Pair your favorite coffee with delectable pastries ,light meals and desserts prepared with the same dedication to quality that's seen in our vehicles. Enjoy a range of mouthwatering options that will satisfy your taste buds, whether you're stopping by for a quick snack or a full meal.



Community and Connection


Refuel isn't just a place to refuel your body; it's a hub for like-minded individuals to connect. We regularly host car shows and meet-ups, bringing together enthusiasts to share stories, knowledge, and the thrill of the open road. This is more than a cafe; it's a pit stop for motor enthusiasts, a place where friendships are forged and passions ignited.



Visit Us Today


you're a car enthusiast, a coffee aficionado, or someone looking for a unique and inviting space, we invite you to join us at refuel. Immerse yourself in a world where vintage racing meets the art of coffee, and where the road never ends. We're more than just a cafe; we're an experience.



Come in, revitalize your senses, and be part of the pit-stop community. We can't wait to share our passion with you.
